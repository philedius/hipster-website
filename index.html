Freerange beans

We are proud to say that each and every one of our coffee beans get to spend at least five hours a day frolicking in the open field while at their stay at the roastery.

Our field

We have 50 acres of open field. The grass on the field was rescued from a building site for an industrial complex. The field has a naturally formed lake for the beans to bathe in.
